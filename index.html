    A&C Application is to simplify and smoothen their Accessioning and Closing procedure. A&C is a web application that replaces their current case management software that helps them convert physical sample information into electronic form and store it as Cases. The cases then move on to Audit and QC where case details and sample details are verified and validated. After sample Quality Check, our application pushes data to Lab Vantage (LAB System) for DNA extraction, then Lab Vantage pushes data to Analysis & Reporting Application where the extracted data is compared and analyzed to generate a test report. This report will be pushed back to our App for Closing, sending out results to customers.
